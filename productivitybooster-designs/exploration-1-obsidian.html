Capabilities

What it does

Built for people who live inside their calendar.

01
Conflict Resolution
Three-layer intelligence: your rules fire first, then AI weighs your patterns, then you decide. Over time, it resolves conflicts without asking.
02
Attendance Tracking
A quiet nudge five minutes after each meeting ends. Did you go? One tap. That answer shapes every future recommendation.
03
Follow-Up Capture
The moment a meeting ends, capture what's next. AI suggests action items based on the meeting context. One tap pushes them to Notion or Jira.
04
AI Decline Messages
Drafts a polished decline using your own templates. Finds a reschedule slot automatically. You approve with one tap — or edit before sending.
05
Behavioral Memory
Every decision you make teaches the system. After 90 days, ProductivityBooster can manage your calendar autonomously — you opt in when ready.
06
Smart Notifications
Reminders 15 minutes before. Nudges after. Weekly digests. The system learns when you need prompting and when you don't — and quiets accordingly.
