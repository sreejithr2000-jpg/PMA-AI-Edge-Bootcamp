What it does

Built for people who
live by their schedule.

A quiet layer of intelligence over your existing calendars. No new workflow to learn. Just less time spent thinking about time.

I
Conflict Resolution
Rules fire first. AI weighs your history second. You decide last — and only when it's genuinely ambiguous.
II
Attendance Tracking
A gentle nudge after each meeting. One tap. That data quietly shapes every future suggestion the AI makes.
III
Follow-Up Capture
AI reads the meeting context and suggests what comes next. Confirm and it pushes to Notion or Jira instantly.

Decline Messages
Drafts a polished message from your own templates. Finds a reschedule slot. You approve — or edit and send.

Reminders that adapt. Less noise for meetings you always attend. More nudges where patterns show you need them.
